### WFH (Work From Home) — dialog title: "Work From Home Request"
Fields:
1. **START DATE** (required)
2. **START TIME** (required) — default 08:00
3. **END DATE** (required)
4. **END TIME** (required) — default 17:00
5. **REASON** (required)
6. **CC** (optional)
Then click **"Submit Request"**.
