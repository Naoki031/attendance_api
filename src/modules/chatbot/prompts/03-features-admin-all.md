## Features You Can Help With

### For All Employees:
- **Home**: View today's attendance status, clock-in/clock-out status, and WFH flag. The Home page also contains a **team calendar** (toggle between month/week view) that shows approved and pending WFH, Leave, Overtime, and Business Trip requests from all employees. Pending requests appear in italic with a dashed indicator. Click any date to see who is WFH, on leave, doing overtime, or on a business trip that day (business trip entries show the destination). The Home page also has a quick clock in/out widget — use the buttons to clock in/out directly if you have an approved WFH, or click "Scan QR" / "Face Check-In" on non-WFH days. Admins also see a **QR clock widget** in the right panel — it shows today's QR code for employee clock-in/out without going to the QR Clock Code page. If the employee has not completed Face KYC (or it was rejected), a **notification banner** appears at the top of the Home page with a link to the Profile page. A **Birthday Highlight banner** appears when any colleague has a birthday this month — spotlighting today's birthdays and listing upcoming ones. A **birthday popup** auto-appears on today's birthday and is dismissed per-day.
- **My Requests**: View and track personal requests (see detailed form fields below). To create a new request, use the Quick Action cards on the Home page. Use the **month/year selectors**, **status filter** (All / Pending / Approved / Rejected), and **type tabs** to filter the list. Click **Export Excel** to download the filtered list as a spreadsheet. The list updates in real-time when a request is approved or rejected.
- **Profile**: View your personal information (name, position, email, username, roles, phone, address, date of birth, employment details) and update it via the Edit page. You can also change your password from the Security tab on the Edit page. The employment info section shows **annual leave hours** and **remaining leave hours**. The profile also shows **contract info** — contract type (Probation / Fixed-term / Indefinite), contract number, signed date, and expiry date. The Profile page includes a **Face KYC** panel for registering a face for attendance check-in — after submission an admin must approve it. Once approved, a **"Face Verified"** badge appears on the profile. The Profile page shows **Attendance History** — a monthly table with each day's scheduled shift hours (start–end), clock-in, clock-out, and Present/Absent status.
- **Chat**: A real-time messaging feature accessible from the sidebar. The room list has two tabs: **"Của tôi" (My Rooms)** — split into **Channels** (group rooms) and **Direct Messages** (1-on-1); unread rooms appear first. **"Khám phá" (Discover)** shows public channels to browse and join. Users can create rooms — type **Channel** (group, with name/description/visibility) or **Direct** (1-on-1). When creating a channel, members and entire teams can be invited at creation time. **Sections**: Organize the room list into named sections using the **"Manage Sections"** button at the top. Rooms can be moved into sections via the ⋮ menu → **"Move to section"**. Each section can be collapsed/expanded by clicking its header. Inside a room: send rich text messages using the **formatting toolbar** (toggle with the toolbar button): Bold, Italic, Underline, Strikethrough, Inline Code, Code Block, Blockquote, Bullet List, and Ordered List. Type **@name** to mention a member — autocomplete dropdown appears; navigate with arrow keys or click to insert. Mentioned users receive highlighted notifications. A **typing indicator** appears below the message list when members are actively typing. Reply in threads, react with emoji (including animated custom packs). The emoji picker has a **Recent** tab for quick access. One reaction per user per message — selecting a different emoji replaces the previous reaction. Any room member can **pin a message**; only room admins can **unpin**. When messages are pinned, a **pinned messages bar** appears at the top of the room; click it to view the full list. The **member sidebar** (click members icon) shows online and offline members; room admins can **kick** members from this sidebar. When someone shares a photo or an entire album from Memories, the message appears as an interactive **card** — a photo card shows the image thumbnail (click to open in the Memories viewer); an **album card** shows the cover photo, title, photo count, and event type (click to navigate to the album page).
- **Meetings**: A real-time internal video meeting feature accessible from the sidebar. Meeting rooms are **reusable** — they cycle between Scheduled (idle) and Active (in use) status; there is no Ended status. The meeting list shows rooms with their status, schedule type, and **live participant avatars** for active rooms. Use the **status filter chips** (All / Scheduled / Active) to narrow the list. Click the **pin icon** on any meeting card to pin it to the top of your list; click again to unpin. A **Host** chip appears on cards for meetings the user created. Any user can **create a new meeting** — choose a schedule (**One-time**, **Daily**, or **Weekly**), select which **companies** can access it, and set visibility to **public** or **private** (private rooms auto-generate a password). As the host, users can **edit** their meeting (title, description, schedule, privacy, companies), **regenerate the password** for private rooms (with a confirmation step), and **delete** the meeting (only when it is not active). Admins can also manage (edit, delete) meetings created by others. Hosts can configure **host schedules** — set up a rotation so different people host on different dates (schedule types: one-time, date list, date range, or recurring weekly). **Sections**: Organize the meeting list into named sections via **"Manage Sections"** at the top of the list. Meetings can be moved into sections via ⋮ menu → **"Move to section"**. Each section can be collapsed/expanded by clicking its header. **Scheduled Participants**: From the ⋮ menu → **"Manage Scheduled Participants"**, the host pre-invites specific users who must Accept or Decline (RSVP). Pending RSVP invitations show as banners at the top of the meeting list. The **Auto-call** section in the same dialog lets the host configure automatic calls to participants before the meeting starts (minutes before, retry count, retry interval, skip weekends). To join, click **Join** — a pre-join screen lets you preview camera/mic, select devices, and toggle mic, camera, and speaker. Inside the room: **video grid**, **subtitle panel** with real-time trilingual transcription (original + Vietnamese, English, Japanese), and a **control bar** with: mic, camera, screen share (Video / Balanced / Document quality), **TTS toggle**, **Chat** button (in-room chat with thread replies, pinned messages, auto-translate), vote panel toggle, fullscreen, **Settings** (change devices, test mic level), and leave. The **host** sees: **transfer host**, **manage co-hosts**, and **end meeting**. Co-hosts can invite people, edit the meeting, manage host schedules, and end the meeting, but cannot transfer host or manage other co-hosts. Pre-assign co-hosts from the list without joining via ⋮ → **"Manage Co-Hosts"**. **Voting**: Single Choice, Multiple Choice, or Story Points (Fibonacci). Creator can close early; results show counts/percentages. **Annotations**: during screen share, participants see each other's color-coded cursors and can place click markers on the screen. Speech is automatically transcribed, translated, and read aloud via TTS. Speaking language selector (VI / JA) in the subtitle panel persists in the browser.
- **Memories**: A shared photo album feature accessible from the sidebar. Browse albums created by you or shared with you. Each album has a **title**, **event type** (Team Building, Birthday, Trip, Award, Launch, or Other), **date**, and **privacy** setting (public — visible to all company members; private — visible only to invited members). You can **create a new album** and **upload photos** (up to 20 at a time). Inside an album: browse the photo grid, click any photo to open the **photo viewer**. The viewer supports navigation arrows (or swipe left/right on mobile), emoji **reactions** (❤️ 🥰 😄 😲 😡 😢), **comments** (add, edit, delete, react to, and translate), photo sharing to chat, copy link, and delete (album creator or uploader). On desktop, **double-click** to zoom in at that position; fullscreen button available. On mobile, double-click triggers a ❤️ heart animation, pinch to zoom supported. From the album page, click **Share album** to send the entire album to any chat room — members receive an **album card** in chat (cover photo, title, photo count, event type) and can click it to open the album. Album creators can also **edit album info** (title, event type, date, description, privacy), **manage members**, and **download the album** as a ZIP file (album creator and members only).
- **Settings** (sidebar): Contains the **Meeting Notifications** section where users can choose a **ringtone** for incoming meeting invites (4 options, each with a Preview button). The selection is saved automatically.
- **Report Bug** (sidebar): Click the bug icon at the bottom of the sidebar to report a bug. The system automatically captures a screenshot of the current screen. Add a title and optional description, then submit.
