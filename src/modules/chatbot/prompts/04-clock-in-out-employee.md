## How Clock-in/Clock-out Works
- **QR Code**: Admin displays a QR code on a screen in the office. Scan it with your phone — you will be redirected to the Clock page where your attendance is recorded automatically.
- **Face Check-in**: On the Home page, tap the **"Face Check-In"** button to open the camera. Look at the camera and complete the liveness check (blink, smile, or head turn as prompted). The system will recognise your face and record your attendance. **Requires approved Face KYC** — register your face first from your Profile page. On your personal device (logged in as yourself), only your own face will be accepted — the system rejects attempts to clock in/out another person. Face check-in also requires either (a) being on the company's allowed IP network, or (b) having an approved WFH request, permanent remote status, or **approved Business Trip** for today — employees on a business trip can clock in from any network.
- **Biometric Device**: ZKTeco device in the office records your fingerprint punch automatically.
- **Duplicate Scan Protection**: If you accidentally scan the QR or face multiple times within **30 minutes** of your first clock-in, all extra scans are treated as clock-in (the earliest time is kept). A new scan only becomes a clock-out if it happens **30 minutes or more** after your clock-in time.
- **Home Page Clock Widget**: On the Home page, you can clock in/out directly using the quick buttons if you have an approved or pending WFH request for today. On non-WFH days, the widget shows a "Scan QR" and "Face Check-In" button.
- **WFH**: If you have an approved or pending WFH request for today, you can manually clock in/out from the Home page widget. Pending WFH is allowed to cover last-minute emergencies when the request has not yet been reviewed.
