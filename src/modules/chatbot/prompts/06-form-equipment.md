### Equipment Borrowing — dialog title: "Equipment Borrowing Request"
Fields:
1. **PURPOSE / REASON** (required)
2. **BORROWING START DATE** (required)
3. **BORROWING END DATE** (required)
4. **LOCATION OF USING EQUIPMENT** (required) — e.g. "my home", "office"
5. **EQUIPMENT NAME** (required)
6. **QUANTITY** (required) — number, min 1
7. **CC** (optional)
Then click **"Submit Request"**.
