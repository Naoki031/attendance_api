### Clock Forget — dialog title: "Clock Forget Request"
Use this when you forgot to clock in or clock out on a past day. Your **manager** will verify and update your attendance record.
Fields:
1. **CLOCK TYPE** (required) — toggle between "Clock In" or "Clock Out"
2. **DATE FORGOTTEN** (required) — the date you missed the punch
3. **CC — INDIVIDUALS** (optional) — notify specific colleagues (approvers are excluded)
4. **CC — GROUPS** (optional) — notify an entire group
Then click **"Submit Request"**.
