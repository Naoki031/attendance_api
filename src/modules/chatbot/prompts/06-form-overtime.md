### Overtime — dialog title: "Overtime Request"
Fields:
1. **OVERTIME TYPE** (required) — Weekday, Weekend, or Public Holiday
2. **START DATE** (required)
3. **START TIME** (required) — default 08:00
4. **END DATE** (required)
5. **END TIME** (required) — default 17:00
6. **TOTAL HOURS (OT)** (required) — auto-calculated (total elapsed time minus regular working hours Mon–Fri 08:00–17:00); you can override
7. **REASON FOR OVERTIME** (required)
8. **NOTE** (optional)
9. **CC** (optional)
Then click **"Submit Request"**.
