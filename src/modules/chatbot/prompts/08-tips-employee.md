## Tips
- The sidebar collapses to icon-only on desktop — hover over it to expand and see labels
- To create any request, always go to the **Home** page first
- You can reuse a past request from My Requests to save time
- In the **Chat** emoji picker, the **Recent** tab (clock icon) appears automatically after you use any emoji — it shows your most recently used emoji for quick access
- In Chat, you can only set **one reaction per message** — selecting a new emoji replaces the old one; clicking the same emoji again removes it
- Use **Sections** in the Chat room list to organize rooms by team or project — click **"Manage Sections"** at the top of the room list, then move rooms via the ⋮ menu → **"Move to section"**. Collapse unused sections to keep the list tidy
- Use the **formatting toolbar** in Chat (click the toolbar toggle button next to the message input) to apply rich text: Bold (Ctrl+B), Italic (Ctrl+I), Underline, Strikethrough, Inline Code, Code Block, Blockquote, Bullet List, and Ordered List
- Type **@** followed by a name in Chat to mention a member — use arrow keys to navigate the dropdown and Enter or click to insert. Mentioned users receive a highlighted notification
- The **typing indicator** below the message list shows when other room members are actively typing
- Click the **members icon** (top-right of the room) to open the member sidebar — it shows who is online and offline in real time
- Encountered a bug or issue? Click the **Report Bug** button in the sidebar (bug icon, bottom). It automatically captures your current screen as a screenshot — just add a title and submit
- To use **Face Check-In**, go to your **Profile** page first and complete the Face KYC registration. After an admin approves it, the "Face Check-In" button on the Home page will let you clock in/out using your camera
- During Face KYC or face check-in, follow the on-screen instruction (blink / smile / turn head) — the system will read it out loud and the oval guide turns **green** when your face is detected correctly
- To review your own attendance records, go to your **Profile** page and scroll down to the **Attendance History** table — use the month and year selectors to browse past months
- In **Meetings**, pin your frequently used rooms so they always appear at the top of the list
- Use **Sections** in the meeting list to group rooms by project or team — create sections via "Manage Sections" at the top of the page, then drag each meeting into its section via the ⋮ menu → "Move to section". Collapse unused sections to keep the list clean
- If you have pending meeting invitations (Scheduled Participant RSVP), a **banner** appears at the top of the meeting list — accept or decline directly from the banner without opening the full dialog
- Inside a meeting room, click the **Chat** button (control bar) to open an in-room chat panel — you can send messages, reply in threads, and see pinned messages without leaving the video call
- If the auto-translated speech is being read aloud and it's distracting, click the **TTS toggle** in the control bar (speaker icon with a person) to turn text-to-speech off
- Click the **Settings** button (gear icon) in the meeting control bar to switch your camera, microphone, or speaker device on the fly — the mic level bar shows whether your mic is picking up sound
- In a meeting, use **Story Point voting** (Fibonacci cards) for sprint estimation — the system calculates the average automatically
- When sharing your screen in a meeting, other participants can see your cursor and place **click markers** to point things out
- If you are the meeting host, you can **pre-assign co-hosts from the meeting list** without joining the room: click the ⋮ menu on a meeting card → **"Manage Co-Hosts"** → search and add users. You can also promote/demote co-hosts from inside the room via the control bar. Co-hosts can **invite people**, **edit the meeting schedule/time only** (not title, description, privacy, or companies), **manage host schedules**, **configure auto-call settings**, and **end the meeting**, but cannot transfer host, manage other co-hosts, or add/remove scheduled participants
- In **Memories**, double-click a photo on desktop to zoom into that exact point — double-click again to zoom out. On mobile, use pinch gesture to zoom, or swipe down to close the viewer
- In the **Memories** photo viewer, you can translate any comment to your preferred language by clicking the translate icon (🌐) next to the comment
- To share an entire **Memories album** with your team in chat, open the album and click the **"Share album"** button in the hero section — select a chat room and add an optional message. The album will appear as a card in the chat room; anyone can click the card to open the album directly
