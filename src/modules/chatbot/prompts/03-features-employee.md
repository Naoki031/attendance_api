## Features Available to You

- **Home**: View today's attendance status, clock-in/clock-out status, and WFH flag. This is also where you submit new requests via Quick Action cards. If you have not yet registered your face (or your KYC was rejected), a **notification banner** will appear at the top of the Home page with a link to your Profile to complete registration. The Home page also contains a **team calendar** that shows approved and pending WFH, Leave, and Overtime requests from all colleagues. You can toggle between **month view** and **week view**. Pending requests appear in italic (with a dashed indicator) so you can distinguish them from approved ones. Click any date on the calendar to open a detail panel showing who is WFH, on leave, or working overtime that day.
- **My Requests**: View all your submitted requests. You can **Edit** pending requests or **Reuse** a past request to submit again. There is no create button here — to create a request go to the Home page.
- **Profile**: View your personal information (name, position, email, username, roles, phone, address, date of birth, employment details) and update it via the Edit page. You can also change your password from the Security tab on the Edit page. The Profile page also contains a **Face KYC** panel where you can register your face for face-based attendance check-in. After submitting, an admin must approve your KYC before face check-in is enabled. If your KYC is rejected, you will see the rejection reason and can re-submit. Once approved, a **"Face Verified"** badge appears on your profile.
- **Chat**: A real-time messaging feature accessible from the sidebar. You can join existing chat rooms or create a new one. Inside a room you can send text messages, reply in threads, react to messages with emoji (including animated custom emoji packs like blob, meow, froge, parrots, and more). The emoji picker remembers your recently used emoji in a dedicated **Recent** tab for quick access. Only one reaction per message per user is allowed — clicking a different emoji replaces your current reaction; clicking the same emoji removes it.
- **Report Bug** (sidebar): Click the bug icon in the bottom of the sidebar to report a bug. The system will automatically capture a screenshot of your current screen and attach it. Add a title and optional description, then submit.
