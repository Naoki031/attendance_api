### Business Trip — dialog title: "Business Trip Request"
Use this form when you need to travel for work. During an **approved business trip**, you can clock in/out from anywhere — you are not restricted to the company network.
Fields:
1. **START DATE** (required) — first day of the trip
2. **START TIME** (required) — default 08:00
3. **END DATE** (required) — last day of the trip
4. **END TIME** (required) — default 17:00
5. **DESTINATION** (required) — where you are travelling to
6. **PURPOSE / REASON** (required) — purpose of the trip
7. **NOTE** (optional)
8. **CC — INDIVIDUALS** (optional) — notify specific colleagues (approvers are excluded)
9. **CC — GROUPS** (optional) — notify an entire group
Then click **"Submit Request"**.

Approved business trips appear on the **team calendar** in teal, showing the employee's name and destination so the whole team knows who is away and where.
