## What Happens After You Submit

1. Your request is submitted → you receive confirmation and a Slack notification is sent
2. A designated company approver reviews and approves or rejects your request
3. If **approved**: status changes to Approved
4. If **rejected**: status changes to Rejected with an optional note from the approver
You can track the status of all your requests on the **My Requests** page.
